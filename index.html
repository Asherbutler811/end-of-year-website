About Our Family

The Butler family has a long and fascinating history that stretches back through 15 generations/more. Our story begins in the early 1100s, when our ancestors were first settled in Britan, Wales, and Scotland. Over the years, the Butler family has been known for resilience, hard work, and a strong sense of community. This can be seen during the crusades through thus motto "As thy find"/"Comme je Trouve". Our Dynesty is shown through the morals of wit and courage.

Butler Family History

Early Beginnings (1800s)

The Butler family roots trace back to rural Ireland during the 1800s. Many members worked as farmers and craftsmen, known for their dedication and skills. Life was simple but built around strong family bonds and a love for the land.

The Move Abroad (1850s)

In the 1850's, some of the Butler family members moved overseas in search of new opportunities. They settled in Christchurch New Zealand, bringing with them their traditions, values, and work ethic. These generations helped build a better New Zealand, while keeping the Butler name alive across the globe.

Modern Generations (2000s - Present)

Today, the Butler family is spread across Britan, New Zealand, Australia, and the United States. We still stay rich in the values of our motto 'As I find'.
